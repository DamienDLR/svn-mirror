l3obsolete
==========

This file lists expl3 commands that have been removed or 
replaced with respect to the version on the TeX Live 2010 disk.

==========

Old						New
---						---

\tl_if_eq:nnTF family	\str_if_eq:nnTF family, with \tl_if_eq:nnTF
						available as a non-expandable test. This change
						reflects the fact that \str_if_eq:nnTF does not
						test category code, whereas the new 
						\tl_if_eq:nnTF implementation does.
